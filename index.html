Welcome to Priya Mushroom Farm.

Your one-stop destination for everything related to mushroom farming.

We offer all types of fresh and organic mushrooms, including oyster, button, milky, and shiitake.

Whether you're a home grower or a commercial farmer, we provide everything you need to grow mushrooms successfully.

From high-quality mushroom seeds (spawn) to growing kits, compost, and humidity control tools, we've got you covered.

Our products are carefully selected to ensure high yield and superior quality.

We also offer training and guidance for beginners in mushroom cultivation.

We aim to support farmers and entrepreneurs by making mushroom farming easier and more profitable.

Visit us today and take the first step toward a sustainable and rewarding farming journey.

Grow healthy. Grow naturally. Grow with us.
